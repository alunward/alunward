Abstracts

A selection of abstract oil and acrylic paintings produced from the studio in Christ Church, Oxford, from 1994 to 2003.

These developed finally into the Goldschmidt series of asteroid paintings, and the Kamarupa series of cloud paintings, since when my work has branched into video and installation work.
